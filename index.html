FAQs

Q: What is BiMi?

A: BiMi is an AI-powered fact-checking assistant. It is designed to help users verify the accuracy of viral news, images, and videos by providing reliable, real-time answers.

Q: How does BiMi verify information?

A: BiMi uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning algorithms to analyse text, images, and videos. It cross-references user queries with a database of verified information from reputable sources, primarily fact-check websites and platforms.

Q: Can I chat with BiMi?

A: Yes, BiMi functions as a chat assistant. You can interact with BiMi through chat, and it will respond to your queries and input.

Q: What types of information can BiMi verify?

A: BiMi can verify various types of information, including viral news, images, and videos. It provides clear verdicts along with detailed explanations and supporting links to accurate information.

Q: How does BiMi ensure the accuracy and transparency of its fact-checks?

A: BiMi’s fact-checking process involves multiple levels of review to ensure reliability. Every fact-checked report is transparently sourced, listing all references and methodologies used.

Q: How does BiMi maintain its commitment to non-partisanship and transparency?

A: BiMi is committed to providing factual, unbiased information. It maintains impartiality and independence by not endorsing any political or partisan viewpoints.
